Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and how we can help transform your business.

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including web development, mobile applications, cloud solutions, AI & machine learning, digital marketing, and UI/UX design. Our team specializes in creating custom solutions tailored to your specific business needs, from initial concept through to deployment and ongoing support.

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while a complex web application or mobile app could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process with regular milestones and deliverables.

We follow an agile development methodology that includes:

  • Discovery & Planning: Understanding your requirements and goals
  • Design: Creating wireframes and visual designs
  • Development: Building your solution with regular updates
  • Testing: Rigorous QA and user testing
  • Deployment: Launch and ongoing support

Yes! We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, feature enhancements, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan that fits your specific needs and budget.

We work with cutting-edge technologies across the full stack. For frontend, we use React, Vue.js, Angular, and Next.js. Our backend expertise includes Node.js, Python, Golang, and Java. We're proficient in cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, and work with both SQL and NoSQL databases. We always choose the best technology stack for your specific project requirements.

Security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard security practices including encryption, secure authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.). All our developers sign NDAs, and we use secure development practices throughout the entire project lifecycle. We also provide security documentation and training for your team.
